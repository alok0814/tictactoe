
How to play
・ Play with Player1 and Player2.
・ First, select the size of the object.
   ( 1: Small, 2: Medium, 3: Large )
・ Next, select the location to place the object in 3x3 squares from 1 to 9.
・ Repeat this process alternately.
・ The winner is the first player to arrange the pieces vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.

Warning
・You can place a large object on top of a small object, but you cannot place a small object on top of a large object.
・ Repeated hits will lead to erroneous operation.
